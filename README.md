Super Market Data Analysis and Insights
This repository contains a comprehensive data analysis project focused on a Supermarket Sales Dataset. The goal is to uncover key business trends, evaluate performance across various dimensions, and provide actionable insights to optimize operations and sales strategy.

The primary objectives of this analysis were to:

Analyze Sales Performance: Determine total sales, average gross income, and profitability over a specific period.

Identify Top/Bottom Performers: Pinpoint the best and worst-performing branches, cities, and product lines.

Customer Segmentation: Understand purchasing behavior across different Customer Types (Member vs. Normal) and Genders.

Time-Based Analysis: Explore sales trends by time of day, day of the week, and month.

Evaluate Payment Methods: Assess the preferred payment methods and their impact on transaction value.
